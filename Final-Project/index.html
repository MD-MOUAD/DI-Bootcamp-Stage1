Cristiano Ronaldo

Football Icon, Global Phenomenon

The Legend

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Throughout his illustrious career, Ronaldo has won numerous accolades including five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes, both records for a European player.

With his exceptional skill, athleticism, and goal-scoring ability, Ronaldo has set countless records and inspired millions around the world. His dedication to fitness, technical excellence, and competitive spirit has made him an icon both on and off the pitch.

Quick Facts

  • Full Name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
  • Born: February 5, 1985 (Funchal, Madeira, Portugal)
  • Height: 1.87 m (6 ft 2 in)
  • Playing Position: Forward
  • Jersey Number: 7
  • International Team: Portugal

Career Highlights

Sporting CP

2002-2003

Ronaldo's professional career began at Sporting CP, where his extraordinary talent quickly caught the attention of top European clubs.

Manchester United

2003-2009, 2021-2022

At Manchester United, Ronaldo developed into a complete forward under Sir Alex Ferguson, winning his first Champions League and Ballon d'Or.

Real Madrid

2009-2018

His time at Real Madrid was legendary, becoming the club's all-time top scorer and winning four Champions League titles.

Juventus

2018-2021

At Juventus, Ronaldo continued his goal-scoring prowess, winning two Serie A titles and becoming the fastest player to reach 100 goals for the club.

Al Nassr

2023-Present

Ronaldo moved to Saudi Arabia's Al Nassr, bringing his star power to a new region while continuing to score at an impressive rate.
